牛津大学赛德商学院Raymond Loveridge教授应邀到管理与经济学院作学术报告
　　应我院应用经济系邀请，牛津大学赛德商学院Raymond Loveridge 教授于9月24日作题为Globalization, Multinational Corporations and National Systems的学术报告。报告由马明主任主持，应用经济系的部分师生到场聆听了报告。
　　Raymond Loveridge教授以经济全球化为背景，从跨国公司和FDI的发展数据进行分析，对垂直的供应链和网状的协调结构做了比较，得出结论认为在新的国际环境下，要求加强国与国之间的合作与联系。
　　整个讲座持续近三个小时，会场座无虚席。演讲结束后，Raymomond Loveridge教授热情回答了同学们的积极提问。此次讲座不仅开阔了老师和同学的学术视野，也是我院应用经济系与国外一流院校的一次精彩对话。最后，Raymond Loveridge教授与参加此次讲座的师生合影留念。
　　Raymond Loveridge is the Professor Emeritus at Sad Business School, University of Oxford, UK. Currently he pursues a number of projects reflecting his interests in multinationals, comparative management, knowledge management and professionalization. Recent books resulting from his research include Information Technology in European Services (1990) and Internationalisation: Process, Context and Markets (1998).
　　He has published widely, including the Internationalization: Process, Context and Markets, Macmillan, Continuity and Crisis in the National Health Service - The Politics of Design and Innovation in Health Care, Information Technology in European Services: Towards a Micro-Electronic Europe, The Strategic Management of Technological Innovation.
